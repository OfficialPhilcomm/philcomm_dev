For Users

The ServiceMarket makes it easy for you to order any competitive Pokemon for PokeMMO you want.

Other than ingame, the Breeders will make you private offers, meaning only you can see them. That makes the market competitive and you get the best prices.

You can keep track of your order status. The breeder who accepts the order can update the status of it.

For Breeders

The ServiceMarket makes it easy for you to find new orders. If you have some free room in the next days, just take a look at the orders overview bok.
You can see all open orders there. Start by making some offers. Keep in mind, you are not the only one giving offers.

Keep the user updated with an order status. The user can see it live.
